2.  **The Problem: The "I Agree" Lie**
    *   People never read Terms & Conditions.
    *   They are intentionally designed to be boring and long (average 50+ pages).
    *   Hidden Dangers: Selling data, forced arbitration, hidden fees, IP theft.
    *   *Stat suggestion*: "91% of people consent to legal terms without reading them."

3.  **The Solution: Agentic RAG**
    *   **Subtext** is NOT just a summarizer. It is an **Autonomous Auditor**.
    *   It uses **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** to "read" the document like a lawyer.
    *   It indexes the entire contract, then "queries" itself for specific risks.

4.  **Deep Dive: The RAG Architecture (The Brain)**
    *   **1. Ingest**: PyMuPDF extracts text page-by-page.
    *   **2. Embed**: Google Gemini Embeddings turn text into "Semantic Vectors".
    *   **3. Index**: We build a local "Knowledge Graph" of the contract in **ChromaDB**.
    *   **4. Reason**: The **Gemini 3 Flash Agent** loops through categories (Privacy, Liability) and retrieves *only* the relevant clauses to analyze.
